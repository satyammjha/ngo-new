Empowering Communities: Our Journey of Hope and Impact...

Kosi Seva Sadan (KSS) is a distinguished not-for-profit organization founded in 1981, inspired by the vision of the esteemed activist and freedom fighter, late Shri Jay Prakash Narayan. With the guidance and support of the eminent social activist, late Shri Prem Bhai of the Sarvodaya Movement, KSS thrived under his dynamic leadership. His efforts led to the initiation of need-based programs for the holistic development of the impoverished and vulnerable sections of society. KSS is duly registered under the Society Registration Act and also holds FCRA Act registration, along with Sections 12(A) and 80(G) compliance under the Income Tax Act.

Currently, KSS operates in the Mahishi, Nauhatta, and SatarKataiya blocks of Saharsha district in North Bihar, focusing its efforts on empowering the marginalized communities. With a strong commitment to its mission, KSS strives to create a positive and sustainable impact on the lives of the underprivileged, working tirelessly to uplift the socio-economic conditions of the region. Through dedicated efforts and strategic programs, KSS continues to honor the legacy of its visionary founders and change-makers, striving to bring about positive change in the lives of those in need.

Geographical Reach:

Geographical Spread of Kosi Seva Sadan (KSS):
Kosi Seva Sadan (KSS) has strategically expanded its operations to reach and impact the lives of marginalized communities in specific regions of North Bihar, India. Currently, KSS is operational in three blocks of Saharsha district, namely Mahishi, Nauhatta, and SatarKataiya. This geographical spread allows the organization to concentrate its efforts and resources on areas that need the most attention and support.

1. Mahishi: Situated in Saharsha district, Mahishi block is one of the primary locations where KSS operates. The organization has a strong presence in this area, focusing on empowering the local communities through various development programs and initiatives.

2. Nauhatta: Another block in Saharsha district, Nauhatta, also benefits from the efforts of Kosi Seva Sadan. Here, the organization works to address the unique challenges faced by the residents and implements projects that promote sustainable development.

3. SatarKataiya: The third block under KSS's purview is SatarKataiya. By extending its reach to this region, KSS aims to uplift the socio-economic conditions of the vulnerable communities, fostering positive change in their lives.

By concentrating its efforts in these specific areas, KSS can effectively assess the needs of the local population and implement targeted interventions that create a lasting impact. The organization's geographic focus enables it to build strong connections with the communities, understand their challenges intimately, and develop contextually relevant solutions. Through this strategic approach, Kosi Seva Sadan continues to work tirelessly to bring about positive change and enhance the overall well-being of the people in North Bihar.
