Accessing Safe Drinking water for all !

We are dedicated to breaking down barriers and transforming lives by addressing critical challenges in water, sanitation, and hygiene (WATSAN) faced by impoverished communities. Our holistic approach tackles discrimination, limited infrastructure geographical constraints, education gaps, and awareness deficits that hinder access to clean water and sanitation services. We understand that clean water is not just a basic need; it's a lifeline, especially in the face of natural disasters.


Our Commitment: Clean Water for Resilient Communities
In the wake of sudden earthquakes, floods, or recurring droughts, access to clean water becomes paramount. KSS recognizes that improving access to cost-effective and sustainable water and sanitation services is essential for preventing the spread of diseases and promoting community well-being.
The Reality: Water Inequity and Health Consequences

India, with over 500 million lacking access to toilets and about 300 million without a safe drinking water source, grapples with an urgent need for change. Poor hygiene practices result in preventable illnesses, including diarrhoea, affecting millions of vulnerable children and mothers and perpetuating malnutrition. At KSS, we are dedicated to altering this trajectory by advocating for improved access to safe drinking water and sanitation for marginalized communities. Our Initiatives: Transforming Lives Drop by Drop Megh Pyne Abhiyan: Our grassroots campaign in Saharsa District, known as the Cloud Water Campaign, champions safe drinking water solutions for flood-prone areas. Groundwater serves as the primary source of drinking water in Bihar, and we collaborate closely with the Public Health Engineering Department to ensure communities have access to clean water and sanitation facilities. Hygiene & Sanitation Access: We work tirelessly to improve water quality and sanitation facilities in 50 villages, focusing on education and behavioral change to ensure long-term impact. Rainwater Harvesting: Through the Megh Pyne Abhiyan program, we promote rainwater harvesting as a sustainable solution to water scarcity. Safe Drinking Water Advocacy: We advocate for the use of safe and pure drinking water, empowering communities to make healthier choices. Low-Cost Sanitation Solutions: We champion the construction of low-cost latrines (Faydemand Souchalaya or Ecosan) and Matka filters, ensuring access to essential sanitation facilities. Water Testing and Treatment: Our comprehensive water testing and treatment efforts guarantee that communities have access to safe and clean water sources. Sustainable Agriculture: We encourage the use of Phaydemand Sauchalaya for responsible use of fertilizers and pesticides in irrigation, safeguarding both water quality and crop productivity. PGWM Program: Our Participatory Groundwater Management (PGWM) program is a critical intervention in the flood plains of the Kosi River in Saharsa District.
